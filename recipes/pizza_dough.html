Classic Pizza Dough

Home

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of lukewarm water
  • 200 grams bread flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar

Steps:

  1. Place warm water in a bowl; add yeast and sugar. Mix for about 10 minutes. Add a spoon of flour in the last few minutes.
  2. Let it stand for about 5 minutes.
  3. Add flour, oil, and salt to the yeast mixture.
  4. Mix it with your hands until you are able to remove it from you hand smoothly and easily.
  5. Leave it grow in a circular shape and cover it, with a cloth.
  6. If using rapid yeast, let it rest a couple of hours, otherwise at least 5 hrs.
  7. Preheat oven to 200 degrees C, and lightly grease you pizza pan with olive oil.
  8. Turn dough out onto a lightly floured surface and pat or roll into a thin circle.
  9. Let it rest for 30 minutes.
  10. Transfer the dough on the pizza pan.
  11. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown.
  12. Remove from the oven and let it cool while preparing your topping from other recipes.
