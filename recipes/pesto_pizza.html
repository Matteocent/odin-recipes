Pesto Pizza Recipe

Home

Ingridients:

  • 1 small of chopped red onion
  • 1 ripe and chopped tomato
  • 1 small cup pesto sauce
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1 small cup of chopped green pepper
  • 1 can of artichokes
  • 1 pre-baked pizza dough
  • 1 can chopped boneless olives, drained

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven once all the ingriedents are ready.
  2. Spread pesto on pizza dough. Top with tomato, bell pepper, olives, red onion, artichoke hearts, and feta cheese.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is melted, up to 10 minutes.
