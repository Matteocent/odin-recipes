Pesto Pizza Recipe

Home

Ingredients:

  • 1 small of chopped red onion
  • 1 ripe and chopped tomato
  • 1 small cup pesto sauce
  • 1 cup crumbled cheese of your choice, mozzarella cheese is a good idea.
  • 1 small cup of chopped green pepper
  • 1 can of artichokes
  • 1 pre-baked pizza dough
  • 1 can chopped boneless olives, drained

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven once all the ingredents are ready.
  2. Spread pesto on pizza dough. Top with tomato, sliced pepper, olives, red onion, artichoke hearts, and cheese.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is melted, up to 10 minutes.
