Meat Feast Pizza Recipe

Home

Ingredients:

  • homemade and pre-baked pizza dough
  • 2 cups of grated cheese (preferably parmisan)
  • 2 skinless boneless chicken breast or legs, already cooked
  • 1 small cup chopped fresh coriander
  • 1 cup as of your favourite spicy barbeque sauce
  • 1 cup sliced peppers
  • 1 cup chopped red onion

Steps:

  1. Once all ingredients are ready. Preheat the oven.
  2. Cut your meat of choice in small cubes, once cooked properly
  3. Place pizza dough on a baking sheet in a tray. Spread your favorite BBQ sauce on dough.
  4. Top with your meat of choice, sliced peppers, onion, and coriander.
  5. Cover with your choice of cheese.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is melted, about 10 to 15 minutes.
